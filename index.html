Summary

I am Data Scientist trained in Economics and an avid learner with a broad background leading and implementing causal and predictive research projects. I aspire to bring this unique blend of econometrics, machine learning, and deep learning skills to build data products.

What I Do

My approach has been to first have a good knowledge of the mathematics behind machine learning and deep learning models, second try to implement them from scratch (when possible), and finally, see how different models perform on the same datasets. In my journey towards becoming a Data Scientist, I have worked on projects to discover customer’s narratives, predicting returns of income from years of education and experience, understanding congenital disabilities concerning Zika in Brazil, finding patterns of crime incidents related alcohol consumption in San Francisco and more.
